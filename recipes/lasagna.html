Lasagna Recipe

    Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses and eggs

    Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasgana according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover the foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
